Who we are

Locally obsessed
Built for Indian operators

From Tier-1 plants to distributed service teams, GearGuard modernizes maintenance with localized compliance, regional observability, and secure tenancy.

Our platform unifies asset registers, maintenance scheduling, work management, AI insights, and governance into a single secure pane. Every tenant is isolated, every action is auditable, and every workflow is optimized for uptime.

Why we exist

Industrial teams deserve consumer-grade UX without compromising on safety, security, or auditability.

Legacy CMMS tools are slow to implement, hard to secure, and frustrating to use. GearGuard delivers a secure-by-default, AI-augmented alternative that teams actually enjoy using.

  • Actionable telemetry and preventive scheduling that prevent downtime.
  • Role-aware access with strong authentication and root governance.
  • Transparent audit trails so compliance teams sleep well.
