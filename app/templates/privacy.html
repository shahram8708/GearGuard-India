Privacy Policy

We protect your data and your trust.

This Privacy Policy explains how GearGuard India collects, uses, secures, and shares information. Our commitment: privacy by design, tenant isolation, and transparent controls.

Updated December 2025
Tenant isolated Data minimization
1. What we collect
  • Account data: name, email, organization details, role, authentication metadata.
  • Operational data: assets, maintenance jobs, schedules, audit logs, and workflow content you create.
  • Technical data: device/browser identifiers, IP address, timestamps, performance signals, and security events.
  • Billing data: payment references via PCI-DSS compliant processors (e.g., Razorpay). We do not store card numbers.
2. Why we collect
  • To deliver the platform, authenticate users, and enforce tenant isolation.
  • To operate maintenance workflows, notifications, AI recommendations, and analytics.
  • To secure the service: detect abuse, investigate incidents, and meet compliance obligations.
  • To improve features and reliability based on aggregated and anonymized usage insights.
3. How we secure data
  • Encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest for supported services.
  • Strict RBAC, MFA enforcement options, audit logs, and least-privilege operational access.
  • Backups, disaster recovery runbooks, and quarterly security reviews.
  • Data residency: primary storage in India regions unless contractually agreed otherwise.
4. Third-party services

We use vetted sub-processors to run the service:

  • Payments: Razorpay or equivalent for subscription charges.
  • Email delivery: transactional email providers for OTP, alerts, and support notices.
  • AI: model providers to generate insights; prompts and outputs are scoped to your tenant and not used to train public models.
  • Monitoring and logging: observability tools to detect reliability or security issues.
5. Cookies & preferences
  • Essential cookies maintain sessions and security controls.
  • Optional analytics cookies (if enabled) are used to improve UX; you can opt out where applicable.
6. Data retention
  • We retain account and operational data for the duration of your subscription and as required for legal or audit purposes.
  • Support requests and security logs may be retained to investigate abuse and improve defenses.
  • Upon termination, data is deleted or anonymized after any agreed export window unless law requires longer retention.
7. Your rights
  • Access and update your profile and organization information.
  • Request export of your organization data, subject to verification and security checks.
  • Request deletion where legally permissible; some records may be retained for audit, billing, or security obligations.
8. AI processing statement
  • AI features are optional and tenant-scoped. Inputs and outputs remain within your tenant boundary.
  • We do not use your prompts or data to train public models. Providers are bound by confidentiality and data protection commitments.
  • AI recommendations are advisory; final decisions remain with your authorized users.
9. Children's data

GearGuard is not intended for children under 16 and does not knowingly collect their personal data.

10. International & GDPR mindset

While we operate primarily in India, we apply GDPR-aligned principles: purpose limitation, data minimization, access controls, and breach notification commitments consistent with applicable law.

11. Updates to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in law, security, or product capabilities. Material updates will be communicated via email or in-product notices.

12. Contact for privacy

For privacy questions or to exercise rights, contact privacy@gearguard.in or submit through our support desk.

We respond to verified requests and may require additional information to protect tenants and users.
